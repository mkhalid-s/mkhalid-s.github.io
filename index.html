About Me

I'm Khalid Shaikh, but people call me "Khalid" or "Mohammad". I'm a full-stack developer by profession based out of Mumbai, India.

I specialize in Java applications along with different front-end stacks such as Angular, React, Swing, CSS, and HTML, that could work with web or non-web applications and platforms. I care about building backend solutions and middleware solutions that can be used in enterprise setups. I'm technology agnostic and working on becoming a cloud evangelist.

My professional life has been purely driven by my passion for design and programming. I remember during my college admissions, how I was so pumped up to opt for the Computer science stream. For the past six years, I'm working in the IT sector with knowledge in domains such as Telecommunications, Digital Communication, Automobile, Construction, Banking, Financial Service and Insurance.

Right now, I'm excited about all the cloud technologies that are growing. I've been working to upskill myself with the leading cloud provider solution and services. Besides learning, I've completed various Cloud certifications on AWS Developer, and Solution Architect at the Associate level. In the following years, I also plan to explore more microservices technologies, decoupling system solutions, distributed, and cloud solutions. And finally, attain a better-rounded full stack developer or Solution Architect both in Cloud and Non-Cloud environments.
